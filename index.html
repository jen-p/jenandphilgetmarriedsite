Our Story

We met at RIT through friends in 2009, but it wasn't until 2010 that we came to know each other and become best friends. After almost a year of bonding over books, movies, video games, and all things sci fi and fantasy, we started dating in 2011. Eight years later, we're still each others' best friends, true loves, badass partners in life, and cat parents. We're finally going to take the plunge.
